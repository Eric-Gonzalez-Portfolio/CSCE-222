\textbf{Example LaTex source for creating a truth table\\[1ex]
Hyunyoung Lee}
\end{center}

To draw the truth table for $p \lor q$ and $p \land q$, you can do the following.

\begin{displaymath}
\begin{array}{|c c|c|c|}
p & q & p \lor q & p \land q)\\
\hline
T & T & T & T\\
T & F & T & F\\
F & T & T & F\\
F & F & F & F\\
\end{array}
\end{displaymath}
